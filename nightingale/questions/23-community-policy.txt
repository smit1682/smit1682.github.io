Q: The Health Survey and Development Committee of 1946, which laid the foundation of India's rural health infrastructure, is popularly known as the
A) Mudaliar Committee
B) Bhore Committee
C) Kartar Singh Committee
D) Srivastava Committee
Ans: B
Topic: Community Health Nursing
Diff: easy
Exp: The Bhore Committee of 1946, or Health Survey and Development Committee, recommended integration of preventive and curative services and the establishment of primary health centres as the basic unit of rural health care.

D) Kartar Singh Committee
Ans: C
Topic: Community Health Nursing
Diff: medium
Exp: The Mudaliar Committee of 1962 assessed the work done since the Bhore Committee. It recommended strengthening existing primary health centres and district hospitals rather than opening new ones, and improving the quality of services.
